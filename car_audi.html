SecondHand Car
A product video for second hand car

dognut

Role

UI/UX Desinger

Tools

Miro for FlowChart
Miro for Wireframe
Illusturator
PhotoShop
Adobe Xd

Duration

7/2021

Concept

This project is a second-hand car commercial film, displaying the functions of the car, Audi A8.

Proposal

According to my client’s request, I designed a motion graphic for their logo, adding elements to show that their products are high-class and useful. The other task was to make a template for the client that could film and edit by themselves in the future.

Film

Before filming, my teammate and I had discussed all the images and shots that we must film, checked the car’s functions that the clients would like to show. On the day of shooting, we set lots of cameras to film easily, and it only took one hour on film works.

Edit

I found it was easier to edit the project by having the script. To make the video present a feeling of high-level and useful, I used different tempos at the beginning, middle, and end of the video.
At the beginning and end of the video, I made motions for the information phase and the logo. I used multiple effect sources to make the logo motions. Then I reset the color and the keyframes of the sources to create unique items.
