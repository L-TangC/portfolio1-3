Dognut

Filter Paper Company Vedio

3D Puzzle

Role

Filming
Editer

Tools

Premiere
After Effect

Duration

5/2017

Concept

This project was a filter paper company's corporate video, using only a short video with descriptions to display the company's quality.

Proposal

The client was really proud for their products, telling me that the experiment of the filter papers in the video was real.
On the other hand, the Japanese were the main users of their products. So I entrusted a Japanese teacher to translate all the text into Japanese.

Film

Our team visited the clients' factory and filmed some senses, machines and the experiment of their product.
We set 3 cameras with different views to film the experiment to catch the images clearly. Amazingly, we completed the filming in only one shot.

Edit

I uses the Japanese video to be the base and edited it with the text script. I used one continuous shot at the experiment part to prove the reality of it. In the middle of the video, I used animation to illustrate the principle of the filter paper.
