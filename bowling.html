Concept

This was a suicide prevention video that I created in the third grade in college which was done with real filming and 2D hand-made animation. ‘Suicide will break parents’ heart’ was the main idea of this project. We won the bronze medal at the Time Young Creative Award for this project.

Process

First, while we had done the storyboard, we filmed a realistic video for our action script.
Second, we created the animation by depicting the actor's motions.
Third, we added some effects to increase the completion of the final video.
