Concept

This is a documentary about Taiwan. The video displays the beauty of Taiwan in 5 minutes.'Everyone loves Taiwan' is our main idea of this project. The breathtaking parts of this small island are not only landscapes but also amazing cultures.

Script

This is a project with my friends. We spent 1 month finishing this project. We planned to visit all around Taiwan and film it. Use a quick cut on editing to preview the beauty of my Country.
We have 8 team members with different skills. So we divide the work for each phase of the project. I am responsible for the film and editing.

Film

I was in charge of the second camera in this project, filming the close-up and side shot are my work.
Controlling the stabilizer was the most difficult part because I have to hold the stabilizer for a long time and switch the lens speedily.

Edit

I handle the style of the editing. While we were on the filming journey, I edited few 1-minute versions for others to choose from that was an important phase to nail down the style for this video. After the filming, we organised all the files with more then 2TB for this project, pick the video clips with other team members, then I would start to edit the longer version with the other editor.
