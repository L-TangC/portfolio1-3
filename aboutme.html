About me

I’m Tang.
A UI/UX Designer
from Taiwan.

I’m an entry UI/UX designer and graduated from Ming Chuan University.

As a human, all the behaviors and the actions are a part of the interactions. Not only with humans, but also anything we touch, feels and see.
As a designer, we solve the problems that humans act with the others. Problem finding, researching, ideating, designing and testing are the way to solve the problem. Improve the products for users, and catch the users smile, always the best thing for me.
