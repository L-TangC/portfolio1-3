Space
Vacuum packing machine

Vepristy Corporate

Role

Dirctor
Filming
Editer

Tools

Premiere
After Effect

Duration

7/2021

Concept

'Space' was a vacuum packing machine for users to store their clothes. This product was also a fund-raising project. It's convenient for users to use not only at home but also at their travels.

Proposal

The client asked us to make a commercial video to put on the fundraiser website. We need to shoot a story to introduce the product to introduce the product.

Script

We wrote the story about a couple using Space to save their relationship.
They tidied away lots of stuff by using Space not only at home but also when they were camping and traveling.

Film

The most difficult part during the filming was we mainly filmed the camping views indoors. We have to shoot in the tent so that the audience cannot see the outdoor scenery.

Edit

Edit phase was the easiest part of this project, the relatively complex part was to correct the color for the film.
