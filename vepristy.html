Concept

This project is a commercial film by interview. We interviewed four people with different jobs, including chef, musician, accountant, and model.
These four videos are all short films describing the experience of using the products.

Edit

To keep viewers interested in the video and not get bored, I shot from various angles and distances.
My editing method was using one main film for the base, and adding other films on it. It's a good way to hide some of the less interesting parts, and give viewers vibrant feelings.
