Concept

This project was my graduation project which was animate with the combination of 2D and 3D.
The story is about 'Dementia, describing an old man who thinks he is still a young boy and has adventures around different places at home.

Ideate

My grandmother was the inspiration for this story. She didn't have Dementia but had similar problems as other elders. At that time, she was about 95 years old, and sometimes she would be suffered from momory confusion. For example, she would look at me and call my father's name.

Design

Combining 2D and 3D animate was the main idea of this project. We decided to draw 2D characters with 3D scenarios, trying many methods to solve the difficulties on the 3D tool's texture. Thus, we decreased the counts of mesh and added stroke for every 3D item so that we could render faster and draw details on UV.

Character

The characters created are all around the protagonist. The old man and the child are the actually same person.The kid is the symbol of the old man's memory and adventure.
His son has been seen as a baboon at the end of the story. The monster is the cockroach from the toilet.

Storyboard

To create a story including realistic activities and dreams. I took reference with a famous animation director Kon Satoshi. He used lots of special transition effects and types to present the interlaced feeling of reality and dream.
I paid tribute to the Kon Satoshi movie in the last part of my animation. I made one continuous shot and drew multiple transition effects, like connecting two takes by eyeball and muzzle to rich my project.
