Ultraman

Soul War

Role

Filming
Editer

Tools

Premiere
After Effect

Duration

7/2019

Concept

This was a record of the Ultraman exhibition for the client to advocate this event. The video displays all the activities on that day.

Proposal

The mission was to film the products on sale, the playgrounds for kids, and the interactions between characters and children. When we were discussing the script, my client gave me some materials as references.

Film

On that day, the client gave me an activity rundown, and I started to filmed before the exhibition started. When the customers came, I shot their actions, like shopping, eating, and playing the rides.
The most important part was to film the Ultraman's characters approaching and taking photos with people.

Edit

I spent the most time in the editing phase because after sending the first version of the film to the client, they expressed their hope that the film could end with another more impactful plot, so I took some time to remake the film.
